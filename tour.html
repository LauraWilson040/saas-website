About Us

Established in 2009, coMATH was created by Angelica Moore and Vernon Lowe.

The purpose of the platform is to give for users the space to Write, post, and collaborate on mathematic formulas.

With over 500,000 registered users to date, coMATH is a leading force in the online mathematics community.
